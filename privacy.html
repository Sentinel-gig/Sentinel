Privacy Policy

Secured Systems Technologies Pvt. Ltd.  ·  Last updated: July 2026

Short version: Sentinel collects your location and driving data only during active shifts, with your explicit consent, to protect your safety. We never sell your data. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Who We Are

Sentinel is a safety intelligence platform operated by Secured Systems Technologies Private Limited ("we", "us", "our"), incorporated in India. Our registered address is Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India.

Contact: business@sentinelco.in

2. What Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, phone number, city
  • Vehicle type and platform (e.g. Ola, Swiggy)
  • Employer/fleet ID (if connected to a fleet)
  • Trusted emergency contacts (names and phone numbers)

Location Data

  • GPS coordinates, speed, and heading — collected every 30 seconds during an active shift
  • Background location — collected while a shift is active, even when the app is minimized
  • Last known location — stored locally for SOS use when network is unavailable

Driving Behaviour Data

  • Accelerometer readings — used to detect harsh braking and rapid acceleration
  • Risk zone crossings — when you enter areas with elevated incident history
  • Driver safety scores — calculated per shift based on the above

Safety Events

  • SOS alerts — triggered manually or via crash detection
  • Incident reports and community surveys you submit
  • Activity logs within the app

Device Data

  • Push notification token — to deliver safety alerts to your device
  • App version and OS type — for debugging and compatibility

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Safety monitoring — detecting crashes, alerting emergency contacts, routing you away from high-risk areas
  • Fleet management — sharing your location, speed, and safety score with your connected fleet operator during active shifts only
  • Community safety — anonymised incident data improves risk zone mapping for all riders
  • App functionality — account management, notifications, and feature improvements

4. Who We Share Data With

  • Your fleet operator — if you have connected your account to a fleet, your live location, speed, and shift activity are visible to your fleet admin during active shifts
  • Emergency services — in the event of an SOS, your location and contact details may be shared with 112 ERSS (India's emergency response system)
  • Trusted contacts — the emergency contacts you designate receive alerts when an SOS is triggered
  • Infrastructure providers — we use Supabase (database), Google Cloud Platform (backend), and Expo (push notifications). These providers process data on our behalf under data processing agreements

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

5. Background Location

Sentinel requests background location access to continue tracking your route when you minimise the app during an active shift. This is essential for crash detection and SOS functionality to work reliably.

Background location is only active when you have started a shift. It stops automatically when you end your shift. A persistent notification ("Sentinel — Shift Active") is displayed whenever background tracking is running so you are always aware.

6. Data Retention

  • Location and tracking data — retained for 6 months, then automatically deleted
  • SOS events — retained for 2 years for safety audit purposes
  • Account data — retained until you delete your account
  • Driver scores — retained for 1 year

7. Your Rights

Under applicable Indian data protection law and GDPR (where applicable), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email us at business@sentinelco.in. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Security

Your data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest. Access to your data is restricted to authorised Sentinel personnel and your connected fleet operator. We conduct regular security reviews of our infrastructure.

9. Children

Sentinel is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has created an account, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our product evolves. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification. Continued use of Sentinel after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

Contact our team at business@sentinelco.in — we aim to respond within 2 business days.
